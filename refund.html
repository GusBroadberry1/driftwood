Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Refund Policy explains your rights regarding payments made to Driftwood, operated by Will Broadberry trading as Driftwood.

Digital products

Driftwood sells digital products — personalised AI-generated travel itineraries. Because each itinerary is generated instantly and uniquely for you upon payment, we operate a no-refund policy once the itinerary has been generated and delivered.

When we will offer a refund

We will offer a full refund in the following circumstances:

  • You were charged but did not receive your itinerary due to a technical error on our part
  • You were charged more than the displayed price
  • You were charged twice for the same itinerary

How to request a refund

If you believe you are entitled to a refund, please contact us at hellodriftwood@gmail.com within 14 days of your purchase, including:

  • Your email address used at checkout
  • The date of purchase
  • A brief description of the issue

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under UK consumer law. If you have concerns about your rights, you can contact the Citizens Advice Bureau or visit gov.uk/consumer-protection-rights.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, please contact us at hellodriftwood@gmail.com.
