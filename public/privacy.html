Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Driftwood ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by Will Broadberry, trading as Driftwood. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

What information we collect

  • Trip preferences you enter in our form (destination, dates, budget, interests, group type, etc.)
  • Quiz answers from the personality quiz
  • Any additional notes you provide
  • Email address and name collected by Stripe during payment
  • IP address and basic browser/device data collected automatically

Payment card details are handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card information directly.

How we use your information

  • To generate your personalised travel itinerary
  • To process your payment via Stripe
  • To improve our service based on how users interact with it
  • To respond to any support queries you send us

Who we share your information with

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Their privacy policy is available at stripe.com/gb/privacy
  • Anthropic — AI itinerary generation. Your trip details are sent to Anthropic's API to generate your itinerary.
  • Vercel — website hosting

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

How long we keep your information

We retain user data for as long as necessary to provide the service, and for up to 2 years thereafter for legitimate business purposes. Payment data is retained by Stripe in accordance with their own data retention policy.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hellodriftwood@gmail.com.

Cookies

Our site uses basic functional cookies required for the site to work, and Stripe uses cookies during the checkout process. By using our site, you consent to this use of cookies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hellodriftwood@gmail.com.
